About This Project

Organic Suite is an open source project designed to empower individuals, organizations, and communities to back down on ads and tracking.

This repository contains a suite of tools and resources, including:

  • amazon without sponsors
  • youtube without ads
  • google without sponsors

Explore the suite, and have the best browsing experience that you ever had!
